<p><i>Melotune</i> is a clean, ad-free Android music streaming app powered by the unofficial JioSaavn API. Inspired by Spotify’s minimal interface, this app enables users to search, stream, and enjoy music seamlessly — all without login or tracking.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>No Ads</li><li>Play tracks in background</li><li>Latest tracks available</li><li>Search for Artists</li><li>Search for Tracks</li><li>Search for Albums</li><li>Search for Playlists</li><li>Doesn't collect any user data</li><li>UI inspired by Spotify</li><li>Auto-updating version display</li><li>Auto-next play functionality</li><li>HTTP/HTTPS support for music CDN domains</li><li>Robust error handling</li><li>Much more...</li></ul>
